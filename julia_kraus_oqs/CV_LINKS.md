`open-quantum-kraus-simulation`

## Best CV format

Use the project title as the visible text and hyperlink it to the repo:

`Open Quantum Kraus Simulation`

**Open Quantum Kraus Simulation** | Julia, ITensors, Tensor Networks, Scientific Computing
